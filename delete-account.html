Delete account

Orbit beta account deletion.

Delete in the app

Open Orbit, go to Settings, choose Delete account, then confirm deletion. This removes your Supabase auth user and user-owned Orbit data.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email privacy@orbit.care from the email address on your Orbit account with the subject "Delete my Orbit account."

What is deleted

Deletion removes your profile, relationships, relationship notes, interaction history, hangout plans, reminder preferences, and account login. Some operational records may remain briefly where required for security, abuse prevention, or service reliability.
